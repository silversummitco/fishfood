## Commercial license terms

A commercial license is available and will require, at minimum:

1. **Permission** — prior written agreement with SilverSummitCo LLC.
2. **Attribution** — visible credit to SilverSummitCo LLC as the originator of

   <https://github.com/silversummitco/fishfood>.
3. **Profit-share** — a fair, mutually agreed share of the profit or revenue
   derived from the use, paid to SilverSummitCo LLC.

## How to request a commercial license

Open an issue at <https://github.com/silversummitco/fishfood/issues> titled
"Commercial license request", or contact SilverSummitCo LLC through the
[silversummitco](https://github.com/silversummitco) account, describing your
intended use. We're happy to work out reasonable terms.
